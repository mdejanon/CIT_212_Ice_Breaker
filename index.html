Why Are the Glaciers melting?

Many of our glaciers have been melting since the early 1900's because of human activities. With the Industrial Revolution and the release of carbon monoxide and green house gasses that have raised the earths temperature.

What We Can Do!

  1. Preparing communities for impacts that are happening now.
  2. Reducing the heat-trapping gases in our atmosphere.
  3. We can switch to renewable sources of energy.
  4. We can conserve energy by better insulating our homes and buildings.
  5. Carpool to school or work.
